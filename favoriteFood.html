Favorite Food

My favorite food is pizza. I love all types of pizza, but being from the Chicago area I am
biased toward deep-dish. I like most toppings but usally stick to the basics.

Favorite Pizza Places

For Deep Dish
- Lou Malnati's: famous Chicago pizzeria serving up my favorite deep-dish pizza. They
have locations in Wisconsin so if you want to try deep-dish I highly recommend them
For Thin Crust
-Ian's: located right here in Madison every student knows about it. Not the greatest
in the world, but great after a long night out. I enjoy their Smokey the Bandit slice.
For Chain Pizza
-Papa John's: inexpensive, fast pizza that is located everywhere and tastes decent.
